Commander

I got into the trading card game Magic the Gathering back in high school through the mobile app, MtG Arena, and eventually learned about the Commander format in College. I've grown to enjoy deckbuilding for this game since there are so many possibilities with the amount of cards and with the company, Wizards of the Coast, releasing new sets pretty frequently. I currently run a Green White Lifegain deck. You can click the link underneath my current commander to see the decklist as well as some other decks I plan to build or currently have started.
