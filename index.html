Who I Am

Hi there, I'm Deric Shaffer, I'm from the Boulder, Colorado area pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science with a minor in Mathematics at New Mexico State University. I began my college career declaring for a degree in Electrical Engineering, but after a year of not being fully sold on it being the right choice for me, I decided to take a step back to find the right choice for me. After remembering the intrigue I had with previous lab assignments, involving Arduino code and a slight bit of VHDL, I gambled and decided to switch over to Computer Science and haven't looked back ever since. During my time in this degree, I've gained a solid foundation in Java, Python, and C, along with experience in Software Development, Web Development, and Data Mining. As of recent, I've been exploring interests in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning while continuing to hone my knowledge in Python and Web Development.
